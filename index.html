    About

    We are a fashion house dealing exclusively in bespoke clothing.

    Our brand has been in the line of exquisite customization of aspired outfits that are handcrafted to perfection since the 1990’s.

    We at She Temple, look forward to giving each outfit a personal and unique touch; something that originally belongs to you. Those distinguishing features that make your clothing unique and special. Customized clothing therefore helps in defining a person’s unique style, taste, body and comfort level.

    We specialize in hand embroidery, stitching and personalized designing and love to keep the Indian, unique hand karigiri/embroidery alive. Whatever the type of embroidery, we can do it all. We have a highly skilled team with decades of experience with us.

    We are a brand that strongly believes, fashion changes but style is personal and permanent. Every human finds and defines beauty differently and all of us are glamourous in our own way be it size, shape or color and therefore clothes should fit us rather than we fitting into them.
